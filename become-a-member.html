Join Us

Join the Community

REP-COM is built on the belief that reliable communication science is a collective endeavour. We invite communication scholars — from PhD students to full professors — who share a commitment to open, rigorous, and reproducible research to join our growing community.

What You Get

What Joining Means

01

Stay Informed

Receive updates on REP-COM projects, findings, and open calls for lab participation. Be notified when new replication initiatives are forming.

02

Participate

Express interest in contributing as a data collection lab in upcoming multilab studies. Joining the community is the pipeline into REP-COM consortium invitations.

03

Shape the Field

Join a network of scholars actively pushing communication science toward stronger evidentiary standards. Contribute to discussions on study selection and replication best practices.

Who Should Join

Open to All Communication Scholars

  • Communication scientists interested in open science and replication
  • Lab teams who could participate in future multilab designs
  • Methodologists and statisticians supporting replication research
  • Scholars who want to stay connected to the initiative's development
  • PhD students and postdocs committed to rigorous methods
No fees — joining is free
